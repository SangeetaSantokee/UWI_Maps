About UWI Maps

UWI Maps is an interactive campus navigation platform designed to help students, staff, and visitors easily find their way around The University of the West Indies campus. Whether you're trying to locate lecture halls, administrative buildings, parking lots, or popular hangout spots, UWI Maps is here to guide you.

Our Goals

  • Make campus navigation simple and accessible.
  • Provide up-to-date location information for all key landmarks.
  • Improve the campus experience for new and returning users.

If you have suggestions, feedback, or spot an issue on the map, feel free to contact us. We'd love to hear from you!
